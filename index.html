What is Escrow Ninja?

 Escrow Ninja is an escrowing service for users of Bitcoin and Monero on the clearnet. When you buy something online from someone you don't know it is hard to gurantee that they will keep their end of the deal especially since Monero is private, Escrow Ninja ensures both parties get the agreed trade. You can see the white paper here. www.escrowninja.net/whitepaper

How are funds secured?

Bitcoin and Monero funds are secured by a non-custodial wallet which you can use to login to the *portal*. Two-step-verifacation via a software app or hardware key is a mandatory to ensure the highest level of security. Each user can choose a username tied to their public key-string, verified on the blockchain.


How does it work?

 Bob wants to buy a keyboard from Alice; Alice is selling a keyboard for 0.3 XMR Bob and Alice uses Escrow Ninja to agree their terms and Bob pays Alice 0.3 XMR but, Alice doesn't recive the money until Bob has recived the keybaord. Escrow Ninja takes a flat fee of 0.5% so Alice gets 0.2985 XMR she can then withdraw it straight away if she wants to or use it to buy something else. Escrow Ninja is* the 3rd party that doesnt't need to be trusted because it is verifiably open-source and uses a non-custodial wallet with the BIP84 native SegWit (P2WPKH) to store the money which admins do not have access to.
